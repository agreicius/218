As mentioned above, the values of sine and cosine at \(\frac{5\pi}{6}\text{,}\) \(\frac{17\pi}{6}\text{,}\) and \(-\frac{7\pi}{6}\) will all be the same, thus
\begin{equation*} \cos\left(\frac{5\pi}{6}\right) = \cos\left(\frac{17\pi}{6}\right) = \cos\left(-\frac{7\pi}{6}\right) = -\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \end{equation*}
