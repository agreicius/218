Theorem 1.16.7. Limit at infinity formulas.

  1. Constant function formula.
    Fix a real constant \(c\text{,}\) and let \(f\) be the constant function \(f(x)=c\) for all \(x\in \R\text{.}\) We have
    \begin{equation} \lim\limits_{x\to \infty} c=\lim\limits_{x\to -\infty} c=c\text{.}\tag{1.82} \end{equation}
