Theorem 1.15.23. Constancy of sign: continuous functions.

Assume \(f\) is continuous on the interval \(I\text{.}\) If \(I\) contains no zeros of \(f\text{,}\) then \(f\) is either always positive or always negative on \(I\text{.}\)
