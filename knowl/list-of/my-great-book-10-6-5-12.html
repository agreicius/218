For example, we can rewrite the expression \(\sin\left(t+\frac{\pi}{2}\right)\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}\sin\left(t+\frac{\pi}{2}\right)\amp =\sin t\cdot\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)+\sin\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)\cos t\\ \amp =\sin t\cdot 0+1\cdot\cos t\\ \amp =\cos t. \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
