Example 1.2.32.

We can solve the quadratic equation \(x^2-4 = 0\) via factoring the difference of squares as before and setting each factor equal to 0:
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}x^2-4 \amp =0\\ (x-2)(x+2) \amp =0\\ x-2=0 \amp \text{ or } x+2=0\\ x=2 \amp \text{ or } x=-2 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
