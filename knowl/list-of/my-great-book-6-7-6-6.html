Example 4.6.10.

The derivative of
\begin{equation*} y = (x^3+4x^2+5)^7 \end{equation*}
is
\begin{equation*} y' = 7(x^3+4x^2+5)^6\ (3x^2+8x) \end{equation*}
where the outside function is \(f(u) = u^7\) with derivative \(f'(u) = 7u^6\) and the inside function is \(u(x) = x^3+4x^2+5\) with derivative \(u'(x) = 3x^2+8x\text{.}\)
