Theorem 2.18.12. Range of Trigonometric Functions.

The six trigonometric functions have ranges described as follows:
\begin{align*} \range \cos \amp =\range \sin =[-1,1] \\ \range \tan \amp =\range \cot = \R\\ \range sec \amp =\range csc = (-\infty, -1]\cup [1,\infty)\text{.} \end{align*}
