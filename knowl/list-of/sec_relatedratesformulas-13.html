Theorem 5.2.13. Volume and Surface Area of a Cone.

A right circular cone of radius \(r\) and height \(h\) has volume
\begin{equation*} V=\frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h \end{equation*}
and surface area
\begin{equation*} S=\pi r\sqrt{r^2+h^2}+\pi r^2\text{.} \end{equation*}
