Definition 1.6.24. Even and odd functions.

Let \(f\) be a function whose domain \(D\) satisfies the following property: if \(x\in D\text{,}\) then \(-x\in D\text{.}\)
  • \(f\) is even if
    \begin{equation} f(-x)=f(x)\tag{1.50} \end{equation}
    for all \(x\in D\text{.}\)
