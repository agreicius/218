Example 5.1.9.

If \(f(x)\) is a differentiable function with derivative \(\frac{df}{dx}\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation*} \frac{d}{dx}[(f(x))^2] = 2f(x)\, f'(x) \end{equation*}
by the chain rule. Likewise,
\begin{equation*} \frac{d}{dx}[(f(x))^3] = 3(f(x))^2\, f'(x) \end{equation*}
