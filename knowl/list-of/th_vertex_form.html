Theorem 1.6.33. Vertex form.

Let \(f(x)=a(x-b)^2+c\text{,}\) where \(a\ne 0\text{.}\) The graph of \(f\) is a parabola with vertex at the point
\begin{equation*} (b,f(b))=(b,c)\text{.} \end{equation*}
The graph of \(f\) extends upwards if \(a> 0\) and downwards if \(a< 0\text{.}\)
