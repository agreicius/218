Definition 8.1.16. Sine and Cosine Functions.

Given any angle \(\theta\) measured in radians and in standard position, let \((x,y)\) be the point where the terminal edge of \(\theta\) intersects the unit circle. The sine and cosine functions of an angle \(\theta\) are the coordinates \(y\) and \(x\) for \(\theta\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \sin\theta = y \qquad \cos\theta = x \end{equation*}
