Theorem 1.10.16. Properties of absolute value.

  1. We have
    \begin{equation} \abs{xy}=\abs{x}\abs{y}\tag{1.62} \end{equation}
    for all \(x,y\in \R\text{,}\) and
    \begin{equation} \abs{\frac{x}{y}}=\frac{\abs{x}}{\abs{y}}\tag{1.63} \end{equation}
    for all \(x,y\in \R\) with \(y\ne 0\text{.}\)
