If you have time, work through the example on your own first, then read the solution below.
  1. We are told that the ice cover starts at \(b=1951\text{,}\) so the point \((0,1951)\) lies on the graph of the function. We are also given the point \((7,1555)\text{.}\) The slope is then
    \begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}m \amp = \frac{1555-1951}{7-0}\\ \amp = \frac{-396}{7}\\ \amp \approx -56.5714 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
    The linear model is then \(A=f(t) = 1951-\frac{396}{7}t\text{.}\)
