Example 9.4.8.

The limit
\begin{equation*} \lim_{x\to\infty}\left(1+\frac{1}{x}\right)^x \end{equation*}
has the form \(1^\infty\text{.}\) After taking \(\ln\) we have the different limit
\begin{equation*} \lim_{x\to\infty} \ln\left(1+\frac{1}{x}\right)^x = \lim_{x\to\infty}x\cdot\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{x}\right) \end{equation*}
