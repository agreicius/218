Theorem 8.3.4. Domain, Range, and Period of the Six Trigonometric Functions.

The six trigonometric functions \(\sin t, \cos t, \tan t, \csc t, \sec t\text{,}\) and \(\cot t\) have the following domain, range, and period:
  • The domain of both \(\sin t\) and \(\cos t\) is the set of all real numbers and their range is \([-1,1]\text{.}\) They are periodic with period \(2\pi\text{.}\)
