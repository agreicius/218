Example 6.6.19.

Remember that when the base \(b\) is not \(e\text{,}\) the derivative has an additional \(\ln b\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}\frac{d}{dx}(3^{x^2+1}) \amp = (\ln 3)3^{x^2+1}\cdot \frac{d}{dx}(x^2+1)\\ \amp = (\ln 3)3^{x^2+1}(2x)\\ \amp = (\ln 3)(2x)3^{x^2+1} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
