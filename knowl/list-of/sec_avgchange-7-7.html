  1. For \(K\text{,}\) we have \(AV_{[1990,2010]}=\frac{K(2010)-K(1990)}{2010-1990}=\frac{602,622-500,631}{20}=5,099.55\text{.}\) For \(W\text{,}\) we have \(AV_{[1990,2010]}=\frac{W(2010)-W(1990)}{2010-1990}=\frac{263,801-187,768}{20}=3,801.65\text{.}\)
