Theorem 2.13.8. Derivative of \(\log_b\).

Let \(b\) be a positive real number. We have
\begin{align} \frac{d}{dx}\log_b x \amp = \frac{1}{x\ln b}\tag{2.51} \end{align}
for all \(x> 0\text{.}\)
