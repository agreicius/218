Example 1.1.18. Union of intervals.

Let \(A=\{x\in \R\mid x\notin [1,2]\}\text{.}\) Express \(A\) as a union of intervals and represent \(A\) graphically on the number line.

\begin{equation*} A=(-\infty, 1)\cup (2,\infty)\text{.} \end{equation*}
