Example 4.4.4.

For example, if we have a position function \(s(t)\text{,}\) then the function is named \(s\) and the independent variable is \(t\text{,}\) so here are all of the different ways we have of writing the derivative:
\begin{equation*} v(t) = s'(t) = \frac{d}{dt}(s) = \frac{ds}{dt} \end{equation*}
where by \(v(t)\) we mean the velocity function.
