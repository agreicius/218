Theorem 2.9.1. Continuity of exponential functions.

Let \(b\) be a positive real number. The exponential function \(f(x)=b^x\) is continuous for all \(x\in \R\text{.}\) As a result, we have
\begin{align} \lim\limits_{x\to c}b^x \amp = b^c\tag{2.8} \end{align}
for all \(c\in \R\text{.}\)
