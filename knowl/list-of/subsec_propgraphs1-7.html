Example 1.2.18.

The \(x\)-intercepts of \(f(x) = 1-3x\) are where \(f(x) = 0\text{,}\) so at the values of \(x\) with \(1-3x=0\text{.}\) Solving for \(x\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}1-3x \amp = 0\\ 1 \amp =3x\\ \frac{1}{3} \amp =x \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
