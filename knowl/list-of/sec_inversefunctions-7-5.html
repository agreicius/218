To find the inverse function \(f^{-1}\text{,}\) we reverse inputs and outputs, meaning we solve \(y=\sqrt{x+1}\) for \(x\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}y\amp = \sqrt{x+1}\\ y^2 \amp = x+1\\ y^2-1\amp = x \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
