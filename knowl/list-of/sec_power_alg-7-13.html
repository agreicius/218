    is defined everywhere but at \(x=0\text{,}\) so its domain is \((-\infty,0)\cup(0,\infty)\text{.}\) Note that the power of \(-2\) is not being applied to the coefficient \(3\text{,}\) only to the \(x\text{.}\)
