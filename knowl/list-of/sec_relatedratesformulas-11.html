Theorem 5.2.12. Volume and Surface Area of a Cylinder.

A right circular cylinder of radius \(r\) and height \(h\) has volume
\begin{equation*} V=\pi r^2 h \end{equation*}
and surface area
\begin{equation*} A=2\pi r h+2\pi r^2\text{.} \end{equation*}
