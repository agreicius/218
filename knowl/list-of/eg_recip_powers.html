Example 1.16.9. Limit at infinity: reciprocal powers.

Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle \frac{\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}-2}{\frac{1}{x^7}+4}\text{.}\) Compute \(\lim\limits_{x\to \infty}f(x)\text{.}\)
